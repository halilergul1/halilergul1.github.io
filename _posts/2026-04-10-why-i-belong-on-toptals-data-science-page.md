### Solving Search Relevance with NLP Classification

At OLX Poland, vehicle category searches suffered from irrelevant results that hurt conversion rates. I owned this problem from start to finish. I conducted exploratory analysis to understand the query distribution, collaborated with product stakeholders to define the success criteria, and curated a labeled dataset of Polish language search queries. I then fine tuned an NLP model for classification, converted it to ONNX for optimized inference, and deployed it on AWS EKS with autoscaling. The production service ran under 200ms latency with Prometheus monitoring and automated health checks. Before going to production, I ran comprehensive load tests to validate throughput and latency under realistic traffic. The A/B test showed a **3% uplift in purchase conversions**, and the model was rolled out to all users.

### Scaling Search Intelligence with LLMs

I also built and deployed an LLM powered query understanding agent that processes over 3 million daily searches at OLX. I load tested the system thoroughly before production rollout to ensure it held up under peak traffic. This system boosted purchase conversions by 1% and reduced the zero result problem by 3%. In parallel, I rebuilt a multi label classification pipeline (using AWS Batch, Airflow, and MLflow) that routes users into more relevant product categories across 7 global markets, delivering a **4% growth in purchase and click conversions**. These projects required strong data engineering, careful pipeline orchestration, and close collaboration with product teams across multiple countries.
